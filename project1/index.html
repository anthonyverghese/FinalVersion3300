WHAT ARE WE GRAPHING?

(X) ENERGY DEFINITION: The intensity of the song on a scale
      from 0 to 1 with 1 being the most energetic.
(Y) VALENCE DEFINITION: Measure of how happy a song makes you
     feel on a scale from 0 to 1 with 1 being the most positive.
(R) POPULARITY DEFINITION: Measure of how well-received a song
     is on a scale of 0-100. The values are mapped to the

     circle's radius.
